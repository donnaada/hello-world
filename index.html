A Collection of some of my favorite desserts

Chewy Oatmeal Cookies

One of my favorite deserts is a homemade thick and chewy oatmeal Raisin cookies filled with craisins, chocolate chips, and walnuts

Some of the things I love about them are:

  • They're Chewy!!
  • They're Chocolatey!
  • They're Oatmeal based and Oatmeals are healthy!!

Boxed Yellow Cake baked in a bundt pan (lol)

I don't know what it is about yellow cake baked in a bundt pan, but for me, it just hits different.

Here are some possible reasons:

  • Easy to cut and slices are easier to eat on the go lol!
  • Bundt cakes look cute with or without icing on top & yellow cake tastes good, so why not combine visual and taste
  • My aunt would bring them to every party we had growing up so for me, it's a nostalgic
